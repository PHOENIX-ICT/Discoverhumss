Virginia Ramirez-Cruz National High School

Siling Bata, Pandi, Bulacan

Skills

Communication Skills

To enhance the communication skills of HUMSS students, focus on the following subjects:

  • Oral Communication (effective speech and public speaking)

  • EAPP (academic writing)

  • Reading and Writing (comprehension and composition)

  • Komunikasyon (Filipino language proficiency)

  • Nonfiction (expressive writing)

    • Critical Thinking

    To develop critical thinking skills, HUMSS students should engage in the following subjects:

  • DISS (understanding societal structures)

  • 21st Century Literature (literary analysis)

  • Statistics and Probability (logical reasoning, data interpretation)

  • Trends, Networks, and Critical Thinking (global patterns)

  • Philosophy (logical reasoning, argumentation)

  • 3 I’s (innovation & interaction)

  • UCSP (culture & society)

    • Research Skills

    To strengthen research skills, students must study the following subjects:

  • Practical Research 1 & 2 (qualitative & quantitative research)

  • Physical Science (scientific inquiry)

  • Entrepreneurship (business strategies)

    • Creativity

    To enhance creativity, HUMSS students should explore the following subjects:

  • 21st Century Literature (modern literary forms)

  • EAPP (technical & creative writing)

  • Earth and Life Science (nature & human interaction)

  • CPAR (Philippine arts)

  • Creative Nonfiction (literary storytelling)

    • Social Awareness

    To improve social awareness, HUMSS students should focus on these subjects:

  • DISS (society & its functions)

  • Philippine Politics and Governance (PPG) (government & policies)

  • Pagbasa (critical reading)

  • Introduction to World Religions and Belief Systems (cultural & religious understanding)

  • Community Engagement, Solidarity, and Citizenship (civic responsibility)

    • Leadership

    To cultivate leadership skills, HUMSS students should participate in the following subjects:

  • PEH 1, 2, 3 & 4 (teamwork & discipline)

  • Personal Development (self-awareness, goal-setting)

  • Culminating Activity (applying acquired skills)

    • Persuasion & Argumentation

    To master persuasion and argumentation, HUMSS students should take these subjects:

  • Oral Communication (public speaking)

  • EAPP (argumentative writing)

  • Reading and Writing (forming strong arguments)

  • Philosophy (logical argumentation)

  • Trends, Networks, and Critical Thinking (debate, discussion)

    • Media & Information Literacy

    To develop media and information literacy, HUMSS students should study these subjects:

  • General Mathematics (data-based reasoning)

  • Statistics and Probability (fact analysis)

  • Empowerment Technologies (EmpTech) (digital literacy)

  • Media and Information Literacy (MIL) (critical media consumption)

    • Time Management

    To improve time management, HUMSS students should practice self-discipline through these subjects:

  • Culminating Activity (project-based learning)

  • Entrepreneurship (business execution)

    • Writing Skills

    To refine writing skills, HUMSS students should focus on these subjects:

  • Creative Nonfiction (literary writing)

  • MIL (journalistic & technical writing)
