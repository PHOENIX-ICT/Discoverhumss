We are 10 Grade 12 students from Virginia Ramirez-Cruz National High School, and we created this website to support students in learning more about the HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences) Strand. Our website provides a complete guide to Grade 11 and 12 subjects, including the essential skills needed to excel in each one. We also offer a career exploration section where students can discover potential career paths and college courses they can pursue after completing the HUMSS strand. Our goal is to make learning more accessible, provide valuable insights, and help students plan for their future with confidence!
